Find the story behind our business

"Good Night" was founded by Malak, a candle lover who believes in the power of heartfelt gifts. Inspired by the warmth and comfort candles bring, Malak started crafting candles as a way to express love and appreciation. Each candle is handcrafted with care, reflecting Malak's dedication to quality and the belief that scents have the power to create lasting memories. Join us in spreading the joy of candles and creating meaningful connections.
